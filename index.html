Who We Are

About CIIFI

The Community Initiative for Insurance and Financial Inclusion (CIIFI) is a non-profit organization based in Tanzania that exists to make insurance and financial education part of everyday life. The organization was established to bridge the gap between financial systems and the communities they are meant to serve.

Many people find financial terms intimidating or inaccessible. CIIFI turns those ideas into simple, relatable lessons that connect with daily experiences, from small market traders to teachers, students, and farmers.

The mission is clear: to empower communities with the knowledge and confidence to make informed financial decisions and protect what matters most.
