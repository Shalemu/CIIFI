Research & Policy Advocacy

Advancing inclusion is a vital mission driven by robust data collection, compelling evidence, and the development of effective policies. Our organization conducts in-depth research to identify and analyze critical gaps in access, affordability, and understanding of insurance services.

By thoroughly examining these disparities, we paint a clearer picture of the challenges faced by individuals and communities. Once our findings are compiled, we proactively share this information with stakeholders, including policymakers, financial institutions, and the general public.

Our advocacy aims to promote essential reforms that ensure fairness and transparency within the insurance industry and beyond. Additionally, we run initiatives designed to empower citizens, equipping them with the knowledge and tools necessary to influence systems that affect their economic well-being.

Through fostering a well-informed populace, we strive to create a more inclusive and equitable financial landscape for all.
