About CIIFI

Inclusive Protection for Every Tanzanian Life

Introduction to CIIFI

Across Tanzania, millions of hardworking youth and women in the informal sector, small-scale farmers, small traders, boda boda riders, and VICOBA members face daily risks that threaten to erase their livelihoods in an instant. A poor harvest, an unexpected illness, or a sudden family tragedy can undo years, if not decades, of perseverance and hard work. At our core, we believe that financial resilience is not a privilege, but a fundamental right for every Tanzanian family.

Our Work & Philosophy

Our multidisciplinary team is deeply rooted in the communities we serve, collaborating closely with local partners to design microinsurance and risk management solutions that truly reflect the unique challenges and aspirations of low-income Tanzanians. By listening to their stories and understanding their realities, we co-create products that are practical, relevant, and accessible. Our advocacy extends beyond product development, we champion the cause of inclusive protection, ensuring that no individual or family is left behind on the path to financial security.

Striking the Right Balance

True and lasting success in microinsurance is achieved when insurers and distributors operate sustainably, fostering a business environment where profitability goes hand in hand with genuine value for low-income customers. We are committed to nurturing this delicate equilibrium, building trust at every level of the value chain, and transforming protection from a mere product into a shared commitment.

Community Resilience 360 (CR360)

Insurance is only one layer of protection in the broader landscape of risk management. True resilience, in our view, stems from a holistic system that empowers households to anticipate and absorb shocks, recover, and adapt in the face of adversity. Our Community Resilience 360 (CR360) model goes beyond insurance to integrate financial literacy, risk awareness, and proactive preventive measures.

The Golden Principle SUAVE

Simple Understood Accessible Valuable Efficient

Microinsurance must be clear, fair, and genuinely useful. We partner with institutions to develop solutions tailored to the diverse needs of low-income communities. Whether it is crop coverage for smallholder farmers, hospital cash plans for market women, or education policies for children, every product delivers tangible value to its users, fostering trust and long-term resilience.

Evolving with People's Needs

Tanzanian communities are ever-evolving, and so are the risks that come with changing lifestyles and an evolving economy. We believe products should grow with people's lives through continuous improvement and innovation.

Building Local Strength

True change happens when knowledge stays within communities. Through our capacity-building programs, we train local institutions, cooperatives, youth, and women in underserved communities to understand, manage, adopt, and promote microinsurance. By transferring skills and tools, we enable our partners to continue expanding access, creating lasting impact from within.
